Highly reliable Authenticator App for Windows

Authenticator App is a highly secure authentication app that can generate 2FA(two-factor authentication) tokens/TOTP(time-based one-time passwords) for Windows users when signing into their online accounts and help them to protect their accounts from hackers by adding an additional layer of security.


Authenticator App can be used with many Bitcoin trading websites as well as games, such as Guild Wars 2, Diablo III, StarCraft, Hearthstone, Heroes of the Storm, Trion/Gamigo, Warframe, and many others.

Authenticator App provides an alternative solution to combine various two-factor authenticator services in one convenient place.

Features

Wide Compatibility

The app is compatible with more than 1000+ websites and services that support two-factor authentication (2FA). It works perfectly with any service that uses Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or other RFC 6238-based TOTP authentication methods.

It also supports games such as Battle.net (World of Warcraft, Hearthstone, Diablo III, GuildWars 2, Glyph, WildStar, Runescape, SWTOR and Steam.

Cross-Platform and Cloud Synced

Authenticator App allows you to securely generate and manage your two-factor authentication (2FA) tokens across all your devices — including Windows PC, smartphones, and tablets.

All your data is seamlessly synchronized through encrypted Google Cloud backup, ensuring you always have access to your codes, no matter which device you're using.

Encrypted Cloud Backup

All your accounts are securely encrypted and backed up to Google Cloud using your own Google account.

This means you can safely restore your data when switching devices or after reinstalling the app — with full end-to-end protection.

Simple Setup

Setting up your 2FA accounts is easy and fast.

Simply scan a QR code or enter your secret key manually to add an account.
The app also supports importing existing accounts from Google Authenticator, making switching effortless.

Advanced Security

Your 2FA tokens are encrypted using strong cryptographic methods and protected by your password or fingerprint.

Only you can access your data. Even if your device is lost, no one else can view or decrypt your accounts.

Organized with Folders

Keep your accounts organized with custom folders and personalized icons.

You can group your 2FA accounts by category, making it easier to find the code you need at a glance.

Browser Integration

With our browser extensions, your 2FA codes can be automatically filled into websites that support 2FA login.

This helps you save time while keeping your accounts secure. Learn more about our browser extensions.

Modern AI Supported: MCP Server/Tools

Supports running as a MCP server, which allows AI agents to access your 2FA codes. Support Cursor, Claude Desktop and other modern AI tools.

Learn more from our MCP Server/Tools page.
